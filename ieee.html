VICE-PRESIDENT OF IEEE

I was the vice-president of SUTD's IEEE student branch from 2017 to 2018, leading and training over 105 student members.

About IEEE

IEEE is the worldâ€™s largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity, and has over 400,000 members worldwide. It also publishes a significant amount of the electrical engineering and computer-science literature in the world.

SUTD's student branch provides internal and external workshops, and provides members funding for the opportunity to learn and try out interesting projects outside of the school's curriculum.

Workshops

We hold internal workshops and classes for IEEE members to teach them about different electronic components including microcontrollers like Arduinos and Raspberry Pis. The skills learnt from these workshops are then incorporated into student initiated projects.

Outside of SUTD, we also organised and assisted in conducting outreach Arduino workshops for various Junior Colleges to use Arduino HC05 Bluetooth module to control microprocessors from external applications in a 2.5-hour long workshop which received positive feedback from both students and teachers.
